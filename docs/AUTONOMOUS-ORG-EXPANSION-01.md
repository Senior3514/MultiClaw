# MultiClaw Autonomous Org Expansion 01

## Principle

The system should be able to study the environment, operate autonomously, and expand the working company structure when needed.

## Product meaning

MultiClaw should not depend only on constant manual pushing.
It should be able to:
- learn the environment
- identify missing operating capacity
- generate additional agents or claws when needed
- route reporting upward through the company structure

## Leadership model

The generated company should feel hierarchical and coherent.
That means the company can include leadership roles such as:
- operator
- CEO-like top leadership
- CTO / systems leadership
- department leads
- specialized claws or agents underneath

## Reporting rule

Raw orchestration noise should be reduced.
Meaningful updates should roll upward through the company structure into clean user-facing reporting.

## Standard

The system should feel like a living company that can grow and adapt as the environment demands.
