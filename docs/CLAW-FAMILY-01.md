# MultiClaw Claw Family 01

## Principle

MultiClaw should be able to sit above a broader claw family, not just exist as one isolated product.

## Family direction

Examples of claw layers or forms may include:
- NanoClaw
- MicroClaw
- OpenClaw
- MultiClaw

## MultiClaw role

MultiClaw is the umbrella layer.
It should be able to orchestrate, organize, generate, or coordinate different claw forms depending on scope and need.

## Product meaning

Not every use case needs the same scale.
Some should be tiny.
Some should be lightweight.
Some should be full company-scale.

MultiClaw should be able to express that spectrum.

## Standard

MultiClaw should feel like the higher-order system that can unify many claw sizes and operating modes.
