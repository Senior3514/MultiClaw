# MultiClaw Company Contact Surfaces 01

## Principle

A generated company should feel reachable.
The user should be able to inspect, steer, and communicate through clear company contact surfaces.

## Direction

Over time, company contact surfaces should include:
- primary operator access
- company overview
- leadership visibility
- artifact access
- channel-based communication surfaces

## Standard

The company should feel contactable and followable, not like a dead folder of generated files.
