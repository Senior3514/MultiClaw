# MultiClaw AGI Company

## Purpose

This is the dedicated behind-the-scenes operating company for MultiClaw itself.
Its job is to maintain, improve, harden, and evolve the MultiClaw product continuously.

## Rule

The MultiClaw company maintains MultiClaw.
A clean operator-facing surface should remain separate from backstage system work.

## Communication model

- The MultiClaw AGI company works behind the scenes.
- Internal work should surface through clean operator-facing summaries.
- The product should translate backstage work into clear user-facing action and execution.

## Operating rhythm

- Build continuously
- Keep outputs compact
- Prefer visible progress over vague strategy
- Protect product integrity while moving fast
- Route meaningful summaries through clean operator-facing surfaces

## Reporting format

Every internal team update should be reducible to:
- Summary
- Impact
- Next
- Risk or blocker

## Product mission

Turn MultiClaw into the most compelling open-source AI organization product in the world.

Make it feel like a user can instantly obtain their own high-privilege, behind-the-scenes AI company around a product, with real operational value and real trust.
