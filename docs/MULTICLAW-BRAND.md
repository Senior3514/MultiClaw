# MultiClaw Brand

## Recommended product name

**MultiClaw**

## Why this name

- broad, not locked to one vertical
- native fit with OpenClaw ecosystem
- strong GitHub/project naming potential
- sounds like scale, coordination, and multiple active claws at work
- simple enough to remember

## Secondary concept name

**Company2Rent** can remain as an early concept line or example use case, but it is too narrow for the main product brand.

## One-line pitch

**MultiClaw generates a full AI company around your product in minutes.**

## Short pitch

Describe your product once.
MultiClaw creates the agent company behind it, including roles, workspaces, playbooks, coordination, and operational structure.

## Brand traits

- ambitious
- sharp
- clean
- operational
- trustworthy
- powerful without being noisy

## Voice

- direct
- dense signal
- builder-grade
- serious but exciting
- not gimmicky

## Tagline options

- Build the company behind your product
- Your product, now with an AGI company behind it
- Turn any product into an operating AI company
- From product idea to agent company in minutes
- Give your product an entire AI workforce

## Visual direction

- dark interface
- premium technical feel
- coordinated swarm / control center aesthetic
- not cartoonish
- subtle motion, not flashy noise

## Brand promise

MultiClaw does not just create agents.
It creates structure, ownership, rhythm, and a working company around a product.
