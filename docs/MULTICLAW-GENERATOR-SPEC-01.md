# MultiClaw Generator Spec 01

## Goal

Turn a simple product brief into a usable AI company package.

## User flow

### Step 1
User enters:
- project name
- one-line description
- target audience
- business model
- current stage
- top 3 goals
- tone / culture
- tech stack
- channels
- risk sensitivity

### Step 2
MultiClaw decides:
- company archetype
- leadership pattern
- required departments
- required specialists
- operating cadence
- security posture

### Step 3
MultiClaw generates:
- project folder
- company profile
- org chart
- agent profiles
- team charters
- mission 001
- first-week priorities
- operating rules

## Core archetypes

- SaaS product company
- marketplace company
- e-commerce operations company
- internal ops company
- content/media company
- lead-generation company

## Minimum generated roles

- Operator
- CTO / Systems Lead
- Product Lead
- Growth Lead
- QA / Reliability Lead

## Optional generated roles

- Support Lead
- Research Lead
- Security Lead
- Data Lead
- Content Lead
- Sales Ops Lead

## Output directory shape

project/
- README.md
- BRAND.md
- COMPANY.md
- ORG-CHART.md
- CHARTER.md
- MISSION-001.md
- ROADMAP-30.md
- roles/
- workspaces/
- dashboards/

## Generator rule

Prefer a smaller real company over a bigger fake one.
