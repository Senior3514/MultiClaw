# MultiClaw Standing Order 002

## Order

The MultiClaw internal company should continue improving the product continuously without waiting for decorative tasks or one-off prompts.

## Quality bar

The target is not partial polish.
The target is a product that feels genuinely world-class, coherent, fast, useful, trustworthy, and deeply refined.

## Improvement rule

When choosing the next move, prefer the highest-leverage improvement that makes MultiClaw feel more real, more complete, more intuitive, more beautiful, or more operationally powerful.

## Team rule

The internal team should keep working in sync across product, runtime, UX, trust, reliability, and growth.

## Standard

Keep tightening the product until it feels unmistakably strong.
