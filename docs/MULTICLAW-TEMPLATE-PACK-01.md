# MultiClaw Template Pack 01

## First template pack

**Product Operating Company**

This is the default pack for founders who already have a product and need an AI company behind it.

## Included structure

### Leadership
- Operator
- CTO

### Core teams
- Product
- Engineering
- Growth
- QA / Reliability

### Optional teams
- Support
- Research
- Security

## Best for

- SaaS
- web apps
- Telegram products
- internal tools
- AI products
- marketplaces in early or growth stage

## What the template must generate

- strong but simple org structure
- project-specific tone
- no generic clone personalities
- actionable week-one mission
- clean ownership boundaries
- compact reporting format

## Required qualities

- useful immediately
- easy to extend
- easy to understand
- compatible with OpenClaw-based workflows

## Not allowed

- random role bloat
- fake autonomy claims
- meaningless ceremonial titles
- generic filler docs
