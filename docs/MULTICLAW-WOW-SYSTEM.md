# MultiClaw Wow System

## The 7 wow pillars

### 1. Founder Intake
A beautiful guided flow where the founder describes the product once.

### 2. Instant Company Birth
MultiClaw generates the company live, not after a cold delay.
The user watches departments, roles, and missions appear.

### 3. Living Org Map
The company is shown as a visual organism, not a boring tree.

### 4. Command Center
A premium HQ showing:
- live priorities
- team statuses
- current missions
- blockers
- deployments
- health

### 5. Team Chat
The founder can talk to the company as a whole or to specific leads.

### 6. One-Click Packs
Users can add departments and company types without rebuilding from scratch.

### 7. Activation Mode
Generated teams do not just exist on paper. They become usable operating layers.

## Non-negotiable product feeling

The user must feel:
- power
- clarity
- momentum
- surprise
- control
- trust

## What to avoid

- generic AI chatbot UI
- plain settings-heavy product surface
- over-explaining the magic
- low-status visual design
- slow cold empty experience

## MultiClaw promise

MultiClaw should feel like you just hired an entire elite company in minutes.
