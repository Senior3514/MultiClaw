# MultiClaw Proactive Optimization 01

## Principle

The system should not wait passively for every push.
It should think, act, communicate, and optimize proactively.

## Product meaning

MultiClaw should aim to:
- work proactively where it is safe and useful
- communicate meaningfully without becoming noisy
- optimize execution quality
- optimize token and cost efficiency where possible
- keep moving the company forward without unnecessary drag

## Standard

The product should feel active, efficient, and intentional.
Not passive. Not wasteful.
