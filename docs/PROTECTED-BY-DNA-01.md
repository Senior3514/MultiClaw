# MultiClaw Protected by DNA 01

## Principle

Protection should not feel bolted on later.
It should be part of the base product DNA.

## Product meaning

MultiClaw should aim for:
- secure defaults
- clean boundaries
- safer exposure posture
- trust-aware runtime behavior
- product surfaces that stay comfortable and intuitive without becoming careless

## Standard

The product should feel convenient and strong at the same time.
Usability and protection should reinforce each other.
