# MultiClaw Terminology 01

## Core naming

### MultiClaw
The overall system: many coordinated claws working together around one product or environment.

### Claw
A claw is an operating unit inside a generated company.
It can represent:
- a team
- a department cell
- a role cluster
- a focused mission unit

## Why it matters

This gives MultiClaw its own product language.
Instead of generic internal naming, the system can speak in its own native terms.

## Usage direction

Examples:
- create a new claw
- assign work to a claw
- inspect active claws
- route this mission to the right claw

## Standard

The language should feel natural, useful, and unmistakably MultiClaw.
