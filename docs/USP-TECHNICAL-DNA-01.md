# MultiClaw USP Technical DNA 01

## Principle

The unique selling proposition of MultiClaw should not live only in branding copy.
It should be visible in the technical shape of the product itself.

## Technical USP direction

MultiClaw should be technically differentiated by combining:
- attach-and-run operation on top of what already exists
- generation of a living company structure instead of a single assistant
- communication with agents from birth
- multi-surface operation across web, CLI, and channel surfaces
- provider-flexible and access-flexible model connectivity
- company hierarchy, autonomy, and expansion inside the runtime

## Standard

The technical architecture should make the product feel unmistakably different, not just described as different.
