# MultiClaw Visibility Model 01

## Core rule

Every generated company should be shaped by the creator's requirements.
That includes:
- departments
- teams
- roles
- seniority levels
- working rules
- permissions
- internal structure

## Organization flexibility

A generated company may include:
- executive leadership
- senior and junior roles
- technical departments
- growth departments
- support teams
- compliance or trust functions
- any custom structure required by the product owner

## Scope rule

Views must be scoped correctly.
A user inside one company should not casually see global internal structures from other companies.
A user inside one department should see the information relevant to that department, unless broader access was explicitly granted.

## Access model

Default posture:
- creator and explicitly authorized users are high-privilege
- company views are company-scoped
- department views are department-scoped
- privileged global views must be intentional, not accidental

## Product implication

MultiClaw should not feel like one messy global org.
It should feel like each generated company has its own real internal structure, with clean boundaries and clear access.

## Standard

No accidental cross-company leakage.
No confusing global visibility.
No fake structure.
Only clean scope, clean ownership, and clean permissions.
