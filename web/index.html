How it works

One product brief. A full AI company behind it.

1. Point at the system

Give MultiClaw the product, repo, machine, or operating domain it should attach to.

2. Deploy the company

MultiClaw creates the company, the claws, the roles, and the operating logic automatically.

3. Steer the army

The company starts operating for the user, while the user keeps the highest control and can inspect, steer, or override everything.

Operating loop

From attachment to living operation.

Attach

Point MultiClaw at the product, repo, app, or machine that already exists.

Birth

Generate the company structure, the claws, the missions, and the operating posture.

Steer

Talk to the company through web, CLI, and future channel surfaces while staying the top-level controller.

Delivery

Move from structure to visible work, inspection, steering, and ongoing operation.

Operator surfaces

Talk to the company directly when you need to.

Web, CLI, and channel-ready interaction should feel native, fast, and clear.

$ multiclaw status
Company online. Routing stable. Missions active.
$ multiclaw ask "what should we fix next?"
Tighten onboarding, verify auth flow, and improve activation.
$ multiclaw create company "AI video studio"
Company created successfully. Claws, missions, and routing generated.
